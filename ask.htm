Mr. Money With The Vibe

Mr. Money with the Vibe is the debut studio album by Nigerian singer Asake,
following his signing with YBNL Nation in February 2022. It was released on
7 September 2022 through Empire Distribution. The album comprises 12 tracks,
which were all produced by Magicsticks.
